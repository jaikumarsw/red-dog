--- 1C. VERIFY MATCH SCORING ALGORITHM ---

The /api/matches/compute endpoint must calculate fitScore (0-100) using:

  agencyType match:       20pts — org.agencyTypes overlaps opp.keywords/category
  geography:              20pts — org.location state appears in opp.keywords
  programKeyword overlap: 25pts — count matches between org.focusAreas and opp.keywords
  deadlineViability:      10pts — days until deadline (>30d=10, 14-30d=7, 7-14d=3, <7d=1, past=0)
  awardSizeFit:           10pts — org budget range vs opp.maxAmount
  timelineAlignment:      10pts — org timeline preference vs deadline urgency
  dataCompleteness:        5pts — how many opp fields are populated

Return full breakdown object, not just total score.
